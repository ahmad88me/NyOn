Introduction back to ToC

Several legal ontologies exists that are created for identifying the legal concepts from the legal documents. As the legal domain relies on both the commonsense and law for analysis and intrepretation, most of the legal ontologies follows the epistomological framework for defining the concepts that manily focuses on commonsense which is used for legal reasoning. There are some onotlogies that are designed to identify the strcuture of th elegal document but then lacks the legal reasoning concepts. This document tries to combine the epistomological concepts along with the legal strcuture and different legal entities which will enhance the legal reasoning and analysis by focusing on kind of entites can be found in which section of the court decision documents which will help to ease the legal reasoning and analysis process.
